1.2  The Ghost Niche: Evolutionary Mismatch 
Evolutionary Mismatch is the scientific theory that our brains, bodies, and instincts evolved for a 
Stone Age world — and are now catastrophically misaligned with modern life. Depression, 
anxiety, loneliness, junk food addiction, phone addiction, burnout, insomnia, comparison culture: 
all are predictable outputs of ancient software running in the wrong environment.

SECTION 6 — THUMBNAIL DOCTRINE 
6.1  Thumbnail Formula 
Zenn's thumbnails use: dark/cinematic background + bold white text overlay + no human face. 
This works because it signals 'documentary' not 'vlog'. For this channel, the formula is the same 
but with a deliberate split visual: 
•​
LEFT SIDE: Ancient / prehistoric imagery (cave, stone tools, fire, savanna, tribe 
silhouette) 
•​
RIGHT SIDE: Modern equivalent (phone screen, city skyline, office cubicle, social media 
notification) 
•​
Text overlay: The provocative question or statement, in bold white or gold, centered or 
along the bottom 
•​
Color grade: The ancient side is warm/sepia, the modern side is cool/neon — the split is 
the message 
•​
NO face needed — the visual contrast IS the hook 
 
6.2  Thumbnail Text Rules 
•​
Maximum 6 words on the thumbnail 
•​
Must create a knowledge gap ('Why You Can't Stop' — stop WHAT? viewer must click) 
•​
Avoid question marks — statements are stronger ('Your Brain Is Still Hunting' > 'Is Your 
Brain Still Hunting?') 
•​
Use one accent color word in gold or red to draw the eye
